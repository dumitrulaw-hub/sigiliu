How it works

Three steps. Sixty seconds. Sealed forever.

01

Upload

Drag any file — designs, documents, code, video. Up to 500MB. Your file never leaves your control.

Features

Instant seal

Upload any file. Get a cryptographic timestamp in seconds. SHA-256 hashing with RFC 3161 legal-grade authority.
